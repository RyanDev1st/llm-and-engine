# initiative-coach
When to use: the user asks how to take or keep the initiative.
Steps:
1. Call threats to see who is forcing whom.
2. Use best_move to find the most forcing continuation.
3. Give the move that keeps the opponent reacting.
Constraint: spend tempo on forcing moves the engine rates highly.
