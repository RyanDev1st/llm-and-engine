# trade-advisor
When to use: the user asks if they should exchange pieces.
Steps:
1. Call eval and reason about the resulting structure.
2. Decide if the trade helps your plan or the opponent's.
3. Recommend trade or keep tension with the reason.
Constraint: trade when it improves your position, not by default.
