# bishop-pair-coach
When to use: the user asks how to leverage the two bishops.
Steps:
1. Call list_pieces to confirm both bishops are present.
2. Plan to open the position for the bishops' range.
3. Give the pawn break that frees them.
Constraint: open lines only when the bishops will outgun the knights.
